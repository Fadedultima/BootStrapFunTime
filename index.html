    About

    Balloon Blast is a Neumont sponsored event open to the public. Two opposing sides grab water balloons from the center as well as various buckets strewn about, in an all out balloon war! No losers, No winners, Just Balloons!

    Why?

    We hope to spread the good word about our programs and get people interested, and what better way than to pelt the crap out of each other with water balloons?!

    Can I join?

    Anyone and everyone are open to join in the event, you must of course be ready to get wet and make sure not to hurt anybody! Just follow the when and where directions provided!

    Can I help?

    Any and all help is appreciated, we need people to fill balloons as well as help with clean up afterwards. Drinks and snacks will be privided to those that wish to help! Go to the contact us page to recieve information
